Yuqi Ding

I am a Senior Engineer at Qualcomm, where I work on Computational Photography, Computer Vision and Computer Graphics.

I obtained PhD degree at Louisiana State University and my advisor is Dr. Jinwei Ye.

Before LSU, I was a Sr. algorithm development engineer at OmniVision Inc.

I obtained MS and BS degrees at Wuhan University.
